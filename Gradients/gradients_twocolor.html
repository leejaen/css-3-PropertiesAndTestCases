gradient

两色线性渐变linear-gradient

语法：

-webkit-linear-gradient([angle | | to keywords，] , 开始颜色值,结束颜色值, [(停靠颜色值,偏移量百分数),...] );

取值

angle:0deg

keywords:[to] [left | right] || [top | bottom] 默认为to bottom

实例

    background-image:-webkit-linear-gradient(#00F,#ff0);
    
    等同代码：
    	 background-image:-webkit-linear-gradient(270deg,#00F,#ff0);
         background-image:-webkit-linear-gradient(top,#00F,#ff0);
         background-image:-webkit-linear-gradient(to bottom,#00F,#ff0);

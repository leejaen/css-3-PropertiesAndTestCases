gradient

线性渐变速度（渐变色宽度）linear-gradient

语法：

-webkit-linear-gradient([angle | | to keywords，] , 开始颜色值,结束颜色值, [(停靠颜色值,偏移量百分数),...] );

取值

angle:0deg

keywords:[to] [left | right] || [top | bottom] 默认为to bottom

实例

        background-image:-webkit-linear-gradient(#f00,#F0F 10%,#ff0);

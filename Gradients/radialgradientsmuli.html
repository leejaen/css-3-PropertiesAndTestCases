半径渐变radialGradient

语法：

-webkit-repeating-radial-gradient([angle | | to keywords，] , 开始颜色值,结束颜色值, [(停靠颜色值,偏移量百分数),...] );

取值

angle:0deg

keywords: [left | right] || [top | bottom] 默认为center

实例

       background-image:-webkit-repeating-radial-gradient(0px 0px,#ff0,#f00 10px,#F0F 10px,#ff0 10px);
